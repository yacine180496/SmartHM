About us

For our project, we want to develop smart home monitoring system that will gave insight about the air and the toxity of the air. Paired to this, we will add gas sensors which are : NO2, CO and C3H8 inside the home at most strategic point that will get data about Gas sensors emission.

The data collected, once centralized, will help us have a better idea on the impact a home have on its environnement.

Luminosity

The light sensor is an electrical sensor that measures light intensity and is also called LDR (light dependent resistor). The sensor gives a signal to the headlights when it gets dark or when it is dark.

Nitrogen dioxide

It is one of several nitrogen oxides. NO2 is an intermediate in the industrial synthesis of nitric acid, millions of tons of which are produced each year for use primarily in the production of fertilizers. At higher temperatures it is a reddish-brown gas. It can be fatal if inhaled in large quantity.

Carbon monoxide

Carbon monoxide (chemical formula CO) is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, flammable gas that is slightly less dense than air. Carbon monoxide consists of one carbon atom and one oxygen atom. It is the simplest molecule of the oxocarbon family. In coordination complexes the carbon monoxide ligand is called carbonyl. It is a key ingredient in many processes in industrial chemistry

Propane

Propane is a three-carbon alkane with the molecular formula C3H8. It is a gas at standard temperature and pressure, but compressible to a transportable liquid.
